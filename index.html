This study explores a systematic and efficient approach to trip planning, leveraging technology and available resources to enhance the overall travel experience. The proposed method integrates destination research, budgeting tools, and itinerary customization to cater to individual preferences. By utilizing digital platforms and travel apps, travelers can streamline the planning process, optimize time and resources, and ensure a well-organized and enjoyable journey. The study aims to provide practical insights into leveraging technology for trip planning, making it accessible and user-friendly for a diverse range of travelers.
